### Equal Likely Model

$$
\begin{aligned}
  \mathcal{L}(0) &= \sum_{i=1}^{N} (y_W ln(P_W(n)) + y_B ln(P_B(n)) + y_C ln(P_B(n))) \\
  &= \sum_{i=1}^{N} (y_W ln(1/3) + y_B ln(1/3) + y_C ln(1/3)) \\
  &= \sum_{i=1}^{N} ln(1/3) \\
  &= ln(1/3) N \\
  &= -525.137
\end{aligned}
$$

  \mathcal{L}(MS) &= \sum_{i=1}^{N} (y_W ln(P_W(n)) + y_B ln(P_B(n)) + y_C ln(P_B(n))) \\

$$
