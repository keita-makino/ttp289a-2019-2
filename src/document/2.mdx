### Equal Likely Model

$$
\begin{cases}
  \mathcal{L}_(0) &= \sum_{i=1}^{N} (y_W ln(P_W(n)) + y_B ln(P_B(n)) + y_C ln(P_B(n)))
  &= \sum_{i=1}^{N} (y_W ln(1/3) + y_B ln(1/3) + y_C ln(1/3))
  &= \sum_{i=1}^{N} ln(1/3)
  &= ln(1/3) N
\end{cases}
$$
